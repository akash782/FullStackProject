BRIJ LITERATURE FESTIVAL

The Brij Literature Festival(BLF) is a magnificent Literature festival in the birth place of Lord Krishna , embodying a rich heritage and a great history of literature. BLF is a beautiful initiative in the form of a two day celebration to promote and preserve the rich heritage of Brij and the nation . The main purpose is to foster and uphold the great Literary and cultural background and portray immense profoundness of the kind of literature Brij actually holds. The event in itself is going to be an extravaganza. This initiative promises to feature a variety of presentation workshops and readings by authors from various walks of life. The festival is going to provide a great opportunity to the participants and audience to learn and admire the beauty of literature.

TEAMS

Design and Development

Graphic design team broadly refers to the tasks associated with developing websites for hosting via internet. The web development process includes web-design, web content development,posters for all the notifications related to the publicity of the team.

Media and Publicity

Media team is responsible for the promotion and publicity of the club in and out of the campus . Moreover, it handles the social media existence of the club. There main motive is to make people aware of the events and workshops and organized by the club.

Finance

Finance Team manages all the bills, expenses and budget-calls of each and every Event that takes place under our domain. The main task for this team is to draft the event within a specified budget.

Event Management

Event Management is amongst the most important teams of our club. The work involved is planning, organizing and conducting all the events and workshops throughout the year . Their job is to keep everyone working together for smooth conducting of the events.

Data Analytics

The crux of Data Analytics is to keep record of all the events , workshops , meetings. So, basically it’s the Journal of The Literario Club. All the details about event , timings , records of participants and winners is facilitated by data team.

Editorial

Editorial Team is the soul of Literario. Being a literary club content has great importance. And so, the main work of Editorial team is to provide content for all the Events and workshops whether its regarding the mails, articles, magazines and a variety of content-driven work.
